Professional **AI-powered Home Energy Management System** (HEMS) for Home Assistant, designed for the Polish energy market.

### 🧠 What It Does

Smarting HOME turns your Home Assistant into an **autonomous energy command center** that optimizes solar production, battery storage, and grid trading — saving you money every day.
